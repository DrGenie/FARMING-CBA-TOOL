Key economic measures

NPV is PV benefits minus PV costs. BCR is PV benefits divided by PV costs. ROI is NPV divided by PV costs. A higher value usually means a stronger economic case, but you should also consider risk, fit with your farm, and practical constraints.

Reminder

The tool does not tell you what to choose. It highlights how each treatment performs financially in present value terms so you can weigh this alongside agronomic and personal factors.

Excel workflow and exports

Keep your detailed CBA in Excel and use this tool as a clean results front-end.

1. Keep full CBA in Excel

Use Excel or another spreadsheet to model annual yields, prices, variable costs, and capital spending for each treatment. Apply your discount rate and horizon to calculate PV benefits and PV costs for every treatment.

2. Paste PV totals here

For each treatment, copy the PV benefits and PV costs from your spreadsheet into this tool’s input table. Use a consistent currency and discount rate across all treatments.

3. Export results back to Excel

You can export the ranked results table as a CSV file and open it in Excel for reports and presentations.
