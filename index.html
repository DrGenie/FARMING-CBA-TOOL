Help

How the tool works and how to keep it flexible without editing Excel.

This tool reads your first worksheet, auto-detects the header row, and builds treatment means. The Mapping tab lets you override what “treatment”, “yield”, and “total cost” mean, without changing your spreadsheet.

The CBA uses incremental yield (t/ha) and incremental cost (/ha) relative to the baseline treatment. Benefits are valued using your grain price assumptions and an effect persistence model (duration and decay). Costs can be timed (Year 1 only, Year 0 only, annual) and can be overridden by treatment.

Use the treatment configuration table to set adoption rates (whole-farm scaling), and to stress-test trial differences using yield and cost multipliers. Export produces a clean workbook for copy-paste into Word and audit trails.
